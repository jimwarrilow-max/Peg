Smarter 🎯

The best window, not the first

Mediocre morning, belter of an afternoon? Peg now says "Hold off till 1pm" — and means it. No more sending you out at 9am into damp disappointment.

Straight-talking 🌦️

Borderline rain, named & shamed

A 35% chance of rain used to be invisible. Now you'll see "⚠️ Note: 35% chance of rain at 2pm" — so the gamble is yours to take, eyes open.

Stricter 🧐

A tougher marker

Anything over a 40% rain chance is now a washout (was 50%), and dead-calm air gets far less credit. Scores run a touch lower on iffy days — on purpose. Fewer false "it'll dry" calls.

Seasonal 🌅

No pre-dawn nonsense

Peg now checks the sunrise. Come winter, it will never recommend pegging out in the dark — the clock starts when the sun does.

Straight-talking ⚠️

Cautious days, useful days

When rain's due late, Peg now points at the good hours — "Best window: 11am–3pm" — instead of just wringing its hands. And it's stopped calling mediocre days "good drying".

Less waffle 🤐

The gloating has ceased

"Nippy, isn't it?" fired on every cold sunny day and wore thin fast. Retired with honours. The numbers speak for themselves now.
